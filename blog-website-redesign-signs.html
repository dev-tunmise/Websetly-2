How to Know When Your Small Business Needs a Website Redesign

Is your website driving conversions or driving customers away? Learn the key warning signs that indicate it's time for a redesign and discover the next steps to transform your online presence.

Your website is often the first impression potential customers have of your business. In today's competitive digital landscape, an outdated or poorly performing website can be the difference between landing a new customer and losing them to a competitor. But how do you know when it's time for a redesign?

Warning Signs Your Website Needs a Redesign

1. Declining Conversion Rates

The most critical sign is when your website stops converting visitors into customers. If you're seeing:

  • Fewer form submissions or quote requests
  • Reduced online sales or bookings
  • High bounce rates and short session durations

These metrics indicate that visitors aren't finding what they need or aren't compelled to take action.

Critical: Text is too small to read, buttons are hard to tap, or content doesn't fit the screen properly.
