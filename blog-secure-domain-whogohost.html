Step-by-Step Guide: Register Your Domain in 5 Minutes

Before You Start

  • Have your preferred domain name ready (with 2-3 alternatives)
  • Prepare your payment method (card, bank transfer, or mobile money)
  • Ensure you have a valid email address for account verification

Step 1: Visit Whogohost

Go to Whogohost's domain registration page and start your domain search.

Step 2: Search for Your Domain

Enter your desired domain name in the search box. The system will instantly check availability across all popular extensions (.com, .ng, .com.ng, etc.).

Step 3: Choose Your Extension

Select the domain extension that best fits your business. For most businesses, .com is recommended for international reach, while .ng extensions are perfect for Nigerian-focused businesses.

Step 4: Add to Cart and Configure

Add your chosen domain to the cart. Select your registration period (1-10 years) and enable privacy protection for added security.

Step 5: Complete Payment

Proceed to checkout and complete your payment using your preferred method. Your domain will be activated immediately upon payment confirmation.

Pro Tip: Register Multiple Years

Register your domain for 2-5 years upfront. This not only saves money but also protects you from price increases and ensures you won't accidentally lose your domain if you forget to renew.
